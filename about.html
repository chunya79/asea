Our Story

Redox technology: the discovery and the legacy

While serving on the board of a small biotech company, ASEA founder Verdis Norton recognised a groundbreaking technology that could supplement our bodies' own potential and support various aspects of well-being.

Deeply inspired by the potential human impact, Verdis acquired this redox technology. The new company was named ASEA — inspired by the constant cycle of rejuvenation and rebirth that comes from the sea.

After extensive research and laboratory testing, ASEA landed on a solution to deliver this technology globally — with limitless impact.
