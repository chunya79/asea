The Fundamentals

What are redox signalling molecules?

Redox signalling molecules are native to your body — created inside every cell as a natural by-product of normal cellular metabolism. They serve as vital messengers, keeping the immune system in balance and facilitating cellular repair and protection.

As we age, our cells produce fewer and fewer of these molecules. The result? A gradual decline in the body's ability to repair and protect itself at the cellular level.

ASEA products are the world's first to contain stabilised, bioactive redox signalling molecules — helping to restore and maintain this critical cellular communication.
